53  long p[] = {0,10,100,1000,10000,100000,1000000,10000000,100000000};

71 NB NB
72 1. k_init();
73  - init kernel but do not start !

75  - msec(int) timertick in msec.From 1 to more than 32000 msec
76  - To reduce timer2 isr load select timertick as high as possible

78 CHECK.
79  - Only check carried out is that the create calls (task, semaphores and mesQs) will not work after k_start.
